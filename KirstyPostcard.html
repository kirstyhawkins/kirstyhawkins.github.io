Kirsty Hawkins

Kirsty Hawkins researches music cognition, specifically the management and organisation of musical information in working memory. Her PhD project is under the supervision of Daniel Mullensiefen. Kirsty is developing a novel music-based measure for prioritisation, with the aim of using this to improve general working memory.

Beyond her Ph.D work, Kirsty has a number of part-time positions both in and outside of academia. Notably, OntoSync is a startup organisation rooted in cognitive psychology that Kirsty works closely with as the Research and Project Delivery Lead. Kirsty also works as a Research Officer for PRS for Music, ensuring music royalties are distributed to musicians timely and accurately. Kirsty uses these experiences as well as teaching roles inform her research.
